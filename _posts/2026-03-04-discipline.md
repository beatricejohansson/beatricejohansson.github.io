I think part of the reason I'm doing this is discipline.

Writing something every day forces me to show up, even when I don't feel like it.

Not every post will be good. Some might be boring. Some might say very little.

But maybe that's the point.

Small thoughts. Small projects. Small steps.

And hopefully, over time, something starts to grow.
