I thought I would be more afraid that no one would read this.

But I’m starting to realize I might be more afraid that someone will.

What if someone I admire finds their way here?  
What if someone who knows much more than I do reads this?

Writing openly about things I find interesting feels personal.  
Maybe even more than that — it feels uncomfortable to write about things I don’t fully understand yet, or things I’ve only just begun to learn.

When you’re still figuring things out, putting your thoughts into words can make them feel more exposed.

I’m not sure I’m comfortable with that yet.

But maybe that’s the point.
