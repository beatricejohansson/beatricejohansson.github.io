Today I decided to try something new: writing every day for the rest of the year.

Honestly, I’m not sure yet what this blog will become. Maybe statistics. Maybe football. Maybe a little bit of both.

I study data analysis, and lately I’ve started noticing how many questions I have — and how easily I forget the answers if I don’t write them down. Most of the time, they are very small thoughts, things that don’t feel important enough to capture.

But often it’s those small thoughts that lead to bigger ideas. And maybe a small blog post is the beginning of something more.
