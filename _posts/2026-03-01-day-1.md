Day 1 of 300 — Daily Notes

It’s 22:30 and I almost didn’t write this.

Today I decided to try something slightly unreasonable: writing every day for the rest of the year. Around 300 posts.

I study data analysis, and lately I’ve started noticing how often curiosity begins with very small thoughts — things that don’t feel important enough to write down.

So this is an experiment in not losing those thoughts.
