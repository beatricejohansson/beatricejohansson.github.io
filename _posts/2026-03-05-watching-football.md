When I watch football on TV, especially when I'm neutral, I start paying attention to different things.

Small details. Movements. Patterns in the game.

But when I watch football live, I'm usually emotionally invested.

I feel the game more than I analyze it.

The noise. The tension. The feeling of being part of something.

I like that football can be both.
Both ways remind me why I love football.
